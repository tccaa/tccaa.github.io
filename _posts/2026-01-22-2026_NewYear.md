The 2026 Lunar New Year Celebration Gala will be held on February 21st (the fifth day of the Lunar New Year) at Chief Joseph Middle School. Come celebrate the Year of the Horse with us! Lunch from 12-2pm is free for TCCAA members (tickets available for purchase for non-members). Performances from 2-4pm are free to all. Everyone is welcome to attend and enjoy the event.

2026 年农历新年联欢晚会将于二月二十一日（正月初五）在 Chief Joseph 中学举行。诚邀您和家人一起来庆祝马年春节！12-2点午餐，三镇华美协会会员免费，非会员需购票。2-4点文艺表演，对所有人免费。欢迎大家莅临观赏。
