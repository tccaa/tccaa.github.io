Join us for a free Community Health Fair hosted by Washington State University medical students! This event will provide health services to the Tri-Cities Chinese-speaking community and help students and healthcare providers develop their medical skills.

**Date:** September 27, 2025
**Time:** 10:00 AM - 12:30 PM
**Location:** Washington State University Tri-Cities Campus, Collaboration Hall

**Health Education Sessions (11:00 AM - 12:00 PM):**
- **Orthopedics:** "Falls & Fractures: Staying Strong, Staying Safe" (骨科：摔跤和骨折)
- **Gastroenterology:** "Indigestion & Digestive Health" (消化科：消化不良与胃肠保健)
- **Oncology:** "Move to Thrive: Exercise is a prescription" (肿瘤科：锻炼为疾病恢复助力)
- **Medical Students:** "No Time? Move Anyway!" Exercise in Real Life (医学生：怎么抽时间锻炼身体)

**Services Provided:**
- Physical Ability & Balance Testing (for 65+) - 65岁以上，测柔软性和平衡力
- Endurance & Muscle Mass Testing (for <64) - 65岁以下，测持久力和肌力
- Public medical education (All in Chinese) - 中文医学科普讲座

请加入我们参加由华盛顿州立大学医学生主办的免费社区健康日！此活动将为三镇中文社区提供健康服务，并帮助学生和医疗提供者发展他们的中文医疗技能。

**日期：** 2025年9月27日
**时间：** 上午10:00 - 下午12:30
**地点：** 华盛顿州立大学三镇校区
